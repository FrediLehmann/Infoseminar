\begin{abstract}
In this paper we will explore the state of \Gls{webrtc} and its use cases. \Gls{webrtc} is a web \Gls{api} for real-time communication on a peer to peer basis. Goal of this work will be to document the state of \Gls{webrtc} and its real world applications. We will explore those possibilities on the basis of example applications and their implementations.
\end{abstract}
